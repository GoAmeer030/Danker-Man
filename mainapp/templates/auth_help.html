Authentication Key

What is authentication key?

 Authentication key is a key which is a combination of your email-id and password that you use for creating your discord account

How to find Authentication Key?

 Step 1:
 Login to your discord account

 Step 2:
 Go to the channel you want Danker Man to work on

 Step 3:
 Press ctrl + shift + i or open inspector tab, There you can see "Network Tab", click on it

 Step 4:
 Send a message in the channel

 Step 5:
 Now, you can see the message request in "Network Tab", click on it

 Step 6:
 Scroll down in the window that opens, under the "Header" section you can see the authentication key
